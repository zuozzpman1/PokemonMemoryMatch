## Download Instructions

### Method 1: KHInsider (Batch Download)
1. Visit: https://downloads.khinsider.com/game-soundtracks/album/pokemon-sfx-gen-1-attack-moves-rby
2. Click "Download" button at top
3. Choose "Download all songs (MP3)" or "Download all songs (FLAC)"
4. Extract ZIP to `/Users/michaelzuo/Desktop/pokemon-sounds/battle/`

### Method 2: The Sounds Resource (Manual)
1. Visit the General Sound Effects page
2. Browse available sounds
3. Click download button for each sound
4. Save to appropriate subfolder

### Method 3: Individual Downloads
1. Visit soundboard sites (101soundboards, SoundsVerse)
2. Play the sound to preview
3. Click download/save button
4. Organize into subfolders
